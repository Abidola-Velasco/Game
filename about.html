About me

Hi, my name is Abi Velasco, I am a freshman and attend ESUMS in New Haven. I like to spend time with my family and friends and I like to play soccer. I also like to play video games, swimming, going on bike rides, and create new things out of legos.

Defenition of a Game

My defenition of a game is as follows: A game is something that one or more people can play wth some sort of objective and requires a bit of skill.

My gamer types

  • My main gamer type is Achiever at 60%. It makes sense because I like to complete all the achievements, acomplish every quest, and collect all the gear.
  • My second gamer type is Killer at 53%. I was surprised because I usually keep to myself in online games. But then again, I do like to ruin someone else's game.(Not very fond of this when it happens to me)
  • My third gamer type is Socializer at 47%. I expected this here as I like to play with my friends that I know I can rely on. But with randoms they are unreliable and don't communicate.
  • My final gamer type is Explorer at 40%. I expected this to be my second highest since I like to explore the open world in games and explore new things.
